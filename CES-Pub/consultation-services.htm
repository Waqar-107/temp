Consultation Services
     

The CES, BUET is capable to provide consultation  services to the public and private sector organizations, and international agencies on energy related issues.

 

 

Approximately 850 graduates in engineering and other disciplines, working in various public and private sector organizations, were trained by the CES on various aspects of conventional and renewable energy technologies in the period 1998-2018.
